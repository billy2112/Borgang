About Us

The Buddhist Committee is dedicated to promoting Buddhist teachings and values in our community. We organize meditation sessions, dharma talks, and cultural events that foster mindfulness, compassion, and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space for everyone interested in Buddhism, whether they are beginners or experienced practitioners. We believe in the universal principles of the Buddha's teachings and work to make them accessible to all.
